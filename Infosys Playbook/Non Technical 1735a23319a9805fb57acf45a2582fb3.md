# **What conflicts/challenges happened in any of your projects?**

**Challenge:**
Our team of 10 lacked project management experience, leading to several issues:

1. **Unclear Task Ownership:**
    
    No designated project manager caused confusion over task assignments and responsibilities.
    
2. **Communication Gaps:**
    
    Without structure, important updates were missed or unclear, leading to misalignment.
    
3. **Large Team, Overwhelming Scope:**
    
    A large team on a big project created coordination issues, making it difficult to stay focused on key deliverables.
    

**Resolution:**
To resolve these, we:

1. **Defined Roles & Responsibilities:**
    
    Assigned clear ownership of tasks using Trello and set deadlines.
    
2. **Improved Communication:**
    
    Introduced weekly standups and ensured everyone updated their tasks on Trello regularly.
    
3. **Set Milestones:**
    
    Focused on high-priority features first, with regular reviews to stay on track.
